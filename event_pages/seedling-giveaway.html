Seedling Giveaway

From sweet Emerald Giants to the blazing Carolina Reaper, our pepper seedling day brought the heat and the heart. Each variety carries its own story, spice, and spirit, ready to take root in your garden. Whether you’re craving a gentle kick or an extra hot adventure, these seeds were chosen to grow flavor, resilience, and joy. Shared with care and rooted in community, there’s a little something here for every kind of grower. 🌶️
