Herb and Tomato Seedlings Giveaway

From spicy Thai basils to heirloom Texas tomatoes, these were the stars of our Seedling Giveaway. Each plant brings flavor, history, and heart to the garden. Grown with care and shared with our community, these seedlings were ready to add joy, color, and delicious harvests to your home. Whether you're just getting started or already love to grow, there's something here for everyone!

These are the plants we shared with love, roots, and purpose!

Italian Basils

Genovese Basil

Classic sweet and aromatic Italian basil.

Napoletano

Big leaves and a bright and sweet flavor.

Pesto

Aromatic and prolific plant selected for pesto production.

Amethyst

A purple-colored variety of Genovese.

Dwarf

A compact sweet basil suitable for growing in small pots.

Lemon

A sweet basil with a bright lemon flavor.

Thai Basils

Lime

Sweet and spicy with a lime flavor.

Mrihani

Aromatic basil with serrated leaves and intense floral taste.

Krapao Red

Classic Tulsi basil with red flower spikes.

Krapao White

Classic Tulsi basil with white flower spikes.

Edible Perennials

Lovage

Aromatic with an intense parsley-like taste; a celery harvested for its leaves.

Skirret

A precursor to modern carrots that divides underground like potatoes.

Einfasche Schnitt

Early parsley cultivar with medieval origins, biennial.

Okras

Indian ‘Bhindi’

Long, slender pods that are smooth and easy to pick.

Iraqi ‘Bamia’

Short, wide pods with a lime green color; small-sized plants.

Tomatoes

Porter

Long, slender pods that are smooth and easy to pick.
